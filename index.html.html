Our Heritage

MORE THAN
A DRINK

Born in 2025 from a single bold idea — that a great drink could carry a feeling, not just a flavor. We started in one small city and grew into a symbol recognized across every continent.

Today, FIZZR exists in over 20 countries, at every celebration, every match, every road trip, every reunion. We are woven into the fabric of human joy.

Our commitment has never changed: craft the most refreshing, most memorable drink on earth — and make it accessible to everyone, everywhere.
